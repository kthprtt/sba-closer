RECOMMENDED: iMazing (Mac or PC)
Step 1: Download iMazing from imazing.com ($40, Mac & Windows)
Step 2: Connect your iPhone via USB cable
Step 3: Click "Messages" in iMazing sidebar
Step 4: Select ALL conversations (Cmd+A or Ctrl+A)
Step 5: Click "Export" → Choose CSV format
Step 6: Save the file → Upload it below
💡 Mac users: iMazing reads your iCloud Messages backup — no need to connect your phone if Messages syncs to your Mac.
💡 PC users: iMazing for Windows works identically. Connect iPhone via USB.
💡 WhatsApp too: iMazing also exports WhatsApp conversations in the same flow.

Step 3: Select ALL conversations
Step 4: Choose CSV format
Step 5: Save or email the file to yourself
Step 6: Upload it below
💡 Alternative: "SMS Backup, Print & Restore" also works (free on Play Store)
